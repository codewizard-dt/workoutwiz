# Future

> A digital personal-training platform that pairs members 1-on-1 with a human (and now AI-assisted) coach who builds and continuously adapts a custom workout program via a mobile app.

## Quick Summary

- **What it is:** A premium remote personal-training subscription. Members hand-pick an expert human coach who designs a custom program, tracks progress (via Apple Watch / Health Connect), and messages daily for accountability. ~$199/month. *(US-only.)*
- **Founded** 2017 in San Francisco by **Rishi Mandal** (ex-Postmates/Google, astrophysicist) and **Justin Santamaria** (ex-Apple, co-creator of FaceTime & iMessage). Raised **~$110M+** across Series A–C (Kleiner Perkins, Trustbridge, SC.Holdings, Founders Fund, Khosla, Optum Ventures, et al.).
- **Major 2025 inflection:** In January 2025 Future **merged with Tom Brady's Autograph** (athlete-engagement company co-founded by Brady and Richard Rosenblatt). The combined company gained a new C-suite — **CEO Ali Jafari**, with **Tom Brady** and **Richard Rosenblatt** as chairmen — and a celebrity-athlete advisory bench (Simone Biles, Usain Bolt, Derek Jeter, Tiger Woods).
- **Product pivot (2025):** The flagship human-coaching product was rebranded **"Future Pro,"** while a new **AI-powered "Future" app** (an AI training engine built on the company's proprietary coaching data + millions of workouts, with selectable coaching "voice personalities") launched in beta — a move that drew mixed reactions from longtime members.
- **Scale signals (company-reported):** 8.2M+ workouts completed, 58M+ coach↔member messages, ~$100M ARR target set for early 2023, and one of the largest full-time professional-coach rosters in the industry.
