# Future — Overview

## Mission & Values
- **Mission:** "To make world-class coaching accessible to more people, so they can build strength, move better, and live healthier, longer lives." — [future.co/about](https://future.co/about)
- **Core belief:** *"People move people"* and *"the right coach changes everything."* The founding thesis: having a coach is the single best predictor of fitness success, yet ~99% of Americans can't afford in-person training ($5k–15k/yr). Future digitizes that relationship to make it affordable and scalable. — [PR Newswire, Series A](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/future-raises-8-5m-in-series-a-funding-led-by-kleiner-perkins-to-digitize-the-personal-training-experience-300855734.html)
- **Founding insight:** ~80% of Americans quit a new exercise routine within ~3 months; the missing ingredient is **accountability**, not information. — [Business of Business](https://www.businessofbusiness.com/articles/future-fitness-app-rishi-mandal-interview/)

## Products & Services
- **Future Pro (flagship):** 1-on-1 remote personal training. Members select an expert coach (many have trained pro athletes / Olympians), receive a custom program, and get daily check-ins + accountability messaging. Workout tracking via Apple Watch / wearables; exercise library with video demos, voice cues, and form guidance. — [future.co](https://www.future.co/)
- **Future (AI app, beta — 2025):** A new AI-powered training engine built on Future's proprietary coaching data and millions of logged workouts, with selectable coaching "voice personalities." — [Athletech News](https://athletechnews.com/age-of-ai-human-personal-trainers-might-become-a-luxury-future-caliber/)

## Business Model
- **Revenue model:** Direct-to-consumer **subscription** (SaaS + human service). Historically **$149/month**; current pricing **~$199/month** with a discounted first month (~$50–$99) and a 30-day full-refund guarantee. — [Forbes Health](https://www.forbes.com/health/weight-loss/future-review/), [future.co](https://www.future.co/)
- **Key customer segments:** Busy professionals and fitness enthusiasts who would consider a personal trainer but want lower cost, flexibility, and accountability without scheduled in-person sessions. **US-only.**
- **Competitive moat:** Largest cohort of full-time professional fitness coaches of its kind; ~90% retention (company-reported); a proprietary dataset of coach↔client interactions and millions of workouts now feeding AI personalization; post-merger access to elite athlete partnerships.
