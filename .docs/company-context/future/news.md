# Future — News & Developments (2019–2025)

## 2025
- **Jan 23, 2025**: Future **merged with Tom Brady's Autograph**, a Web3 / athlete-engagement company co-founded by Brady and Richard & Dillon Rosenblatt (2021). The deal combines Future's coaching platform with Autograph's athlete relationships and AI/wearables ambitions; Richard Rosenblatt becomes Executive Chairman. — [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/01/23/tom-bradys-autograph-merges-with-digital-fitness-company-future/), [Athletech News](https://athletechnews.com/tom-bradys-autograph-future-merge/)
- **~Q1–Q2 2025**: Leadership transition — **Ali Jafari** named CEO; new VP-level executive team; Tom Brady & Richard Rosenblatt installed as chairmen; elite-athlete advisory bench added (Simone Biles, Usain Bolt, Derek Jeter, Tiger Woods).
- **~Q1–Q2 2025**: Product split — flagship human-coaching app rebranded **"Future Pro"**; new **AI-powered "Future" app** launched in **beta** (AI engine trained on proprietary coaching data + millions of workouts, with selectable coaching voice personalities). Mixed/critical member reaction over use of coaching-conversation data. — [Athletech News](https://athletechnews.com/age-of-ai-human-personal-trainers-might-become-a-luxury-future-caliber/), [Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/futurefitapp/comments/1qyngfb/future_changing_to_future_pro_and_beta_ai_seeming/)
- **Apr 2025**: PCMag adds **Future Pro** to its recommended workout-apps list. — [PCMag](https://www.pcmag.com/picks/best-workout-apps)
- **~May 2025**: **Android app** released, ending iOS exclusivity. — [Better Living](https://onbetterliving.com/future-app/)

## 2024
- CEO Rishi Mandal appears on Bloomberg with 2024 fitness-industry predictions, emphasizing AI: *"The biggest winners in an AI world are the ones who have the largest, most proprietary data sets."* — [Future LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/future-research-inc)
- Continued positioning as a hybrid human + digital coaching leader as the broader fitness-app market consolidated post-pandemic.

## 2023
- **Apr 2023**: Forbes profile — "How The Training App, Future, Has Cracked The Code Of Fitness," featuring CEO Rishi Mandal on accountability + technology. — [Forbes](https://www.forbes.com/sites/omaidhomayun/2023/04/16/how-the-training-app-future-has-cracked-the-code-of-fitness/)
- Company-stated target to surpass **$100M ARR by early 2023**.

## 2022
- **Feb 3, 2022**: Closed **$75M Series C** led by SC.Holdings & Trustbridge Partners (with Kleiner Perkins, Optum Ventures), bringing total funding to **$110M+**. Added celebrity investors **Kate Hudson, Oliver Hudson, J.J. Watt, Rory McIlroy, Kevin Durant/Rich Kleiman's Thirty Five Ventures**; the Hudsons agreed to produce a podcast series for Future. — [PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/digital-fitness-coaching-service---future---raises-75m-in-series-c-funding-301474827.html), [Fortune](https://fortune.com/2022/02/03/kate-hudson-backed-digital-fitness-company-future-raises-75-million-in-funding/)

## 2020
- **Oct 16, 2020**: Raised **$24M Series B** led by Trustbridge Partners (Caffeinated Capital, Kleiner Perkins). Growth tripled amid the pandemic at-home-fitness boom; ~90% retention reported. — [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2020/10/16/future-raises-24m-series-b-for-its-150-mo-exercise-app-amid-at-home-fitness-boom/)

## 2019
- **Series A: $8.5M** led by Kleiner Perkins, "to digitize the personal training experience." — [PR Newswire](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/future-raises-8-5m-in-series-a-funding-led-by-kleiner-perkins-to-digitize-the-personal-training-experience-300855734.html)

## Earlier
- **2017**: Future founded in San Francisco by Rishi Mandal and Justin Santamaria; first coach reportedly hired via Craigslist. — [Business of Business](https://www.businessofbusiness.com/articles/future-fitness-app-rishi-mandal-interview/)
