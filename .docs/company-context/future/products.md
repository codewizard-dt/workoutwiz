# Future — Products & Services

## Product Catalogue

| Product / Plan | Description | Target Customer | Pricing |
|----------------|-------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Future Pro** (flagship) | 1-on-1 remote personal training: hand-pick an expert human coach who builds a custom program, tracks progress via wearable, and messages daily for accountability. Adaptive programming, exercise library with video demos / voice cues / form guidance. | Fitness enthusiasts & busy professionals who want a personal trainer's accountability at lower cost and full schedule flexibility. | ~**$199/month**; discounted first month (~$50–$99); 30-day full-refund guarantee. *(Originally $149/mo.)* |
| **Future** (AI app, beta, 2025) | AI-powered training engine built on Future's proprietary coaching data + millions of workouts; generates personalized programs with selectable coaching "voice personalities." | Users wanting lower-cost, AI-driven personalization (vs. dedicated human coach). | Beta — pricing not fully public *(unverified)* |

## Platform & Integrations
- **Native integrations:** Apple Watch (core to the original product — health-tracking + workout verification); **Apple Health** and **Google Health Connect** (heart rate, exercise data, unified health profile across devices/apps). — [Google Play listing](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.future.future)
- **Platforms:** iOS since launch; **Android added ~May 2025** (previously iOS-only). — [Better Living review](https://onbetterliving.com/future-app/)
- **Communication layer:** In-app messaging + FaceTime check-ins between coach and member (leveraging co-founder Santamaria's iMessage/FaceTime background).
- **Data strategy:** "Future unifies your health data from all fitness apps and devices into one comprehensive coaching platform" — eliminating data silos to power personalized coaching and (now) AI recommendations.

## Customer Use Cases

| Customer / Vertical | Problem Solved | Outcome |
|---------------------|----------------|---------|
| Busy professionals | No time/budget for scheduled in-person training; routines don't stick | Flexible, coach-adjusted programs; company cites ~90% retention and "98% report increased consistency within 4 weeks" |
| Lapsed exercisers / beginners | Restart safely, build a sustainable habit | Coach paces progression (e.g., ramping from 0 → 4+ days/week) with ongoing accountability |
| Travelers | Maintaining a routine away from home gym | Coach builds travel-specific / equipment-flexible workouts on request |
| App-store reviewers | Generic class/streaming apps (Peloton, OrangeTheory) didn't drive adherence | 4.9/5 App Store rating; testimonials citing 5–15-month adherence milestones |

## Recent Product Launches & Changes *(last ~18 months)*
- **Jan 2025**: Merger with Tom Brady's **Autograph** — adds athlete partnerships, wearables, and AI ambitions. — [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/01/23/tom-bradys-autograph-merges-with-digital-fitness-company-future/)
- **~Q1–Q2 2025**: Flagship rebranded **"Future Pro"** (human coaching) and a separate **AI-powered "Future" app** launched in **beta** — built on proprietary coaching data + millions of workouts, with selectable coaching "voice personalities." Drew mixed/negative reaction from longtime members over data use and "yet another AI coaching app." — [Athletech News](https://athletechnews.com/age-of-ai-human-personal-trainers-might-become-a-luxury-future-caliber/), [Reddit r/futurefitapp](https://www.reddit.com/r/futurefitapp/comments/1qyngfb/future_changing_to_future_pro_and_beta_ai_seeming/)
- **~May 2025**: **Android app** released, ending iOS exclusivity.
- **Apr 2025**: Added to PCMag's recommended workout-app lineup as "Future Pro." — [PCMag](https://www.pcmag.com/picks/best-workout-apps)

## Roadmap Signals
- **AI + human hybrid** is the stated direction: "combining software, wearables, expert coaching, and AI to help individuals unlock their full potential" (Rosenblatt, on the merger).
- **Expansion beyond fitness** into broader **health coaching** — a stated use of Series C capital ("Fitness is only the beginning… the larger picture of personal health").
- **Leveraging proprietary data for AI**: former CEO Mandal's thesis — "the biggest winners in an AI world are the ones who have the largest, most proprietary data sets."
- **Athlete-driven GTM**: using Autograph's relationships with athletes and partners for growth.

## GTM Motion
- **Free tier / trial:** No free tier; **discounted first month** (historically $1 via partner promos, commonly ~$50–$99) + 30-day refund guarantee.
- **Self-serve vs. sales-led:** **Self-serve, D2C subscription** — sign up in-app, pick a coach, start.
- **Deal size:** ~$199/month per member (~$2,400/yr) — premium positioning, deliberately not mass-market.
